NAC3 voice-driven ads

An ad that speaks to you, listens, fills the form against a NAC3 contract, and closes the sale to a CRM. Zero typing, zero clicking required. The lead lands as a record + a testimonial email at the seller within seconds.

What changes about advertising

For 40 years, ads have been one-way: the brand shows you something, you click, you read, you fill a form, maybe you buy.

These ads talk back. They propose a product, hear what you say, match it against the seller's data model (declared as a NAC3 manifest), fill the order, and submit it. The lead arrives at the CRM the instant you say "yes".

Under the hood: each ad is a NAC3 plugin. The fields, the verbs, the submit action -- all dispatchable by a human voice, by a screen reader, by an LLM agent, by a Playwright spec. Same contract, same surface.
